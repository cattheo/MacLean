MacLean: Products

Products

MacLean provides a family of functors which construct products. For two values \(x:5\) and \(y:7\) the resulting value is 

prod : (5,7)  -> 35 
       (x,y) = x * 7 + y
with two projections projections: 
  prj0 : 35 -> 5  
         x = z udiv 7
  prj1 : 35 -> 7 
         y = z umod 7
The notation 5 * 7 is commonly used to represent the product. Note that the term denotes the constructor, as will as the two projections: the actual type targetted by the constructor is just \(32\) which is also the domain of the prohections.
