We begin by considering the family of \(\verb|Data<r>| \) full subcategories of the distributive category \(Set\) whose objects, or types, are sets consisting of integers in the rannge \(0..n-1\), where \(n \lt r\). This category has finite products and sums up to the representation size \(r\). Within a specific data category, when it is unambiguous, we shall denote the type of 5 elements by
